1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 38.8 introduces Autonomous Execution Governance Intelligence.

This layer becomes the execution-governance and execution-boundary engine
of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Ensure that autonomous execution remains controlled, explainable,
traceable and aligned.

Primary Mission:

Determine:

* what may proceed
* what requires confirmation
* what requires review
* what exceeds authority
* what should be blocked
