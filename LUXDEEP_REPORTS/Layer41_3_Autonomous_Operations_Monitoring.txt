1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 41.3 introduces Autonomous Operations Monitoring.

This layer becomes the operational monitoring and oversight engine
of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Continuously observe operational progress and operational health.

Primary Mission:

Detect:

* milestone delays
* schedule drift
* operational bottlenecks
* phase completion issues
* dependency blockage
* continuity degradation

before they impact the operation.
