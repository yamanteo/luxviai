1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 41.5 introduces Autonomous Operations Governance.

This layer becomes the governance and authority engine of LuxCode
operations.

Primary Goal:

Ensure all operations remain within approved authority,
risk and governance boundaries.

Primary Mission:

Determine:

* whether an operation is permitted
* whether an operation requires approval
* whether an operation exceeds risk limits
* whether escalation is required
* whether continuation is allowed
* whether suspension is necessary

before operational progression.
