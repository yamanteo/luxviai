1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 38.7 introduces Autonomous Execution Decision Intelligence.

This layer becomes the decision-selection engine of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Choose between multiple possible execution paths.

Not merely generate plans.

Not merely simulate outcomes.

Decide.

Primary Mission:

Determine:

* which path should be selected
* which strategy should be preferred
* which risks are acceptable
* which tradeoffs are justified
* which execution route maximizes success probability
