1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 41.4 introduces Autonomous Operations Continuity.

This layer becomes the long-duration operational continuity engine
of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Maintain operational stability across days, weeks and long-running
project cycles.

Primary Mission:

Ensure:

* operations remain active
* milestones remain connected
* schedules remain coherent
* dependencies remain valid
* operational intent remains preserved

despite interruptions and time gaps.
