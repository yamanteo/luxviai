1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Layer 33.2 introduces Failed Change Intelligence Preview — a read-only analysis
system focused on what failed, why it failed, repeated failed approaches,
recurring dead ends, historical failure patterns, failure chains, and loop
detection.

The layer is SYSTEM-CENTRIC and TECHNOLOGY-AGNOSTIC, supporting Python, HTML,
CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, JSON, YAML, Database, Infrastructure, API,
Workflow, and Documentation.

Ten failed change types are profiled with structured analysis, including
loop detection and avoidance recommendations.
