1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 35.8 introduces Engineering Autonomy Intelligence Preview.

This layer becomes the autonomy planning engine of LuxCode.

Goal:

Determine how much autonomy is appropriate for a given task.

Not maximum autonomy.

Not minimum autonomy.

Appropriate autonomy.

Core Principle:

The safest level of autonomy that still achieves the objective.
