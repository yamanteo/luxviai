1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 40.8 introduces Agent Execution Recovery Coordinator.

This layer becomes the execution recovery and continuation coordination
engine of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Coordinate safe recovery when execution deviates from expectations.

Primary Mission:

Determine:

* how to recover
* how to continue
* how to reroute
* how to rollback
* how to minimize loss
* how to preserve continuity

after execution problems occur.
