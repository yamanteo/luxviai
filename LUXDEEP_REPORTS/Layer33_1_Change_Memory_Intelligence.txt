1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Layer 33.1 introduces Change Memory Intelligence Preview — a read-only analysis
system that tracks what changed, why it changed, what succeeded, what failed,
recurring patterns, historical similarities, and cross-system effects.

The layer is SYSTEM-CENTRIC and TECHNOLOGY-AGNOSTIC, supporting Python, HTML,
CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, JSON, YAML, Database, Infrastructure, API,
Workflow, and Documentation.

Ten change types are profiled with structured analysis.
