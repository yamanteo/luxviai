1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 41.2 introduces Autonomous Operations Scheduling.

This layer becomes the operational scheduling and timeline coordination
engine of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Transform operational plans into coordinated schedules.

Primary Mission:

Determine:

* when phases begin
* when milestones occur
* when dependencies activate
* when resources are needed
* when operations should pause
* when operations should resume

across long-running operational timelines.
