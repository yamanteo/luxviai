1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Layer 32.4 introduces Root Cause Intelligence Preview — a read-only analysis
system that detects the likely origin of failures across the LUXDEEP runtime.
It separates symptom from cause, identifies trigger chains, and highlights
probable source systems.

Eight root cause categories are profiled with structured findings, probable
causes, contributing factors, dependency links, and trigger chains.
