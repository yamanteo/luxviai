--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Layer 33.6 introduces Verification Intelligence Preview — a read-only analysis
system for the LuxCode Verification Engine.

Verification Model:
  REAL_SYSTEM → MASTER_CLONE → WORKING_CLONE → SANDBOX → VERIFICATION
  → DELIVERY_CANDIDATE

Verification Gates:
  sandbox_gate | dependency_gate | integration_gate
  workflow_gate | regression_gate | production_validation_gate

Delivery Rule:
  Delivery Candidate may only be created if ALL verification gates pass.

  Any FAIL condition blocks Delivery Candidate.
