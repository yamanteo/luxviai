1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 41.8 introduces Autonomous Operations Supervisor.

This layer becomes the operational oversight and supervision authority
of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Continuously supervise operational coordination, stability and strategic
alignment.

Primary Mission:

Detect:

* operational drift
* priority drift
* resource conflicts
* governance violations
* continuity degradation
* strategic misalignment

before they impact operational outcomes.
