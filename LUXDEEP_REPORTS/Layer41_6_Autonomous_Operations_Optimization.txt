1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 41.6 introduces Autonomous Operations Optimization.

This layer becomes the operational efficiency and optimization engine
of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Continuously improve operational performance without changing
operational intent.

Primary Mission:

Identify:

* operational inefficiencies
* schedule inefficiencies
* resource waste
* execution bottlenecks
* avoidable risks
* optimization opportunities

and generate improvement recommendations.
