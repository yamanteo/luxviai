1. OVERVIEW

---

Layer 40.7 introduces Agent Execution Supervisor.

This layer becomes the execution oversight and monitoring engine of LuxCode.

Primary Goal:

Observe execution quality, stability and alignment during runtime.

Primary Mission:

Detect:

* execution drift
* stalled execution
* blocked execution
* escalating risks
* unexpected behavior
* coordination failures

before they become major failures.
