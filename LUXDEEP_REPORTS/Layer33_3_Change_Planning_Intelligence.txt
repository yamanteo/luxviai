1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Layer 33.3 introduces Change Planning Intelligence Preview — a read-only
analysis system that generates dependency-aware, risk-aware, and rollback-aware
change plans. It is the first SYSTEM CHANGE PLANNING engine for LuxCode.

Each plan includes a recommended strategy, alternative strategies, explicitly
avoided strategies (from Layer 33.2 failed change data), required files,
affected files, dependency chain, risk/complexity/effort estimates, validation
steps, and a rollback strategy.

Ten plan types are profiled.
